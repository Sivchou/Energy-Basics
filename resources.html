How Electricity Bills Actually Work

Electricity bills are based on how much energy you use, measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh). The more devices you use and the longer they run, the higher your bill becomes.

Easy Ways Students Can Reduce Energy Costs

Simple habits like unplugging unused devices, using LED lighting, and minimizing heating or cooling usage can significantly reduce monthly electricity costs.

Energy Efficiency Basics (Explained Simply)
